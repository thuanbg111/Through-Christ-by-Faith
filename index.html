THROUGH CHRIST BY FAITH: A NON PROFIT ORGANIZATION

We are a faith‑driven 501(c)(3) nonprofit serving our neighbors with dignity. Our specific purpose is to provide for the homeless and less fortunate, and empower girls and women through practical assistance and mentorship.

Learn more See programs

What We Do

Compassion in action for people facing hardship.

  • Weekly food outreach & care packages
  • Hygiene kits, clothing, and resource referrals
  • Empowerment workshops and mentoring for girls & women

Food & Essentials

Groceries, hot meals, and hygiene kits for individuals and families in need.

Outreach & Referrals

Street outreach with clothing support and connections to shelters and services.

Empowerment

Mentoring and workshops that build confidence, wellness, and life skills for girls & women.
